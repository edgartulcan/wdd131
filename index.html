Guayaquil, Ecuador

Ecuador (in Kichwa, Ecuador; in Shuar, Ekuatur), officially the Republic of Ecuador (in Kichwa, Ecuadorpi Ripuwlika; in Shuar, Ekuatur Nunka), is a sovereign country located in the northwestern region of South America. It is a social, democratic, plurinational state governed by the rule of law, with a unitary, decentralized presidential republic as its form of government. Its capital is Quito, and its most populous city is Guayaquil.
